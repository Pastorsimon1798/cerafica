# Cerafica Content Strategy

> Unified playbook for what to post, when to post, and why — across Instagram, Journal, email, and website.
> Version: 1.0.0

---

## 1. Content Mission

**Every post must do at least one of three things:**
1. **Sell a piece** (direct revenue)
2. **Teach a process** (saves, shares, authority)
3. **Reveal the maker** (trust, differentiation, loyalty)

If it does none of these, don't post it.

---

## 2. Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Finished Work (30%)
**Goal:** Drive sales and showcase the catalog.

- New drops
- Restocks
- One-of-one features
- "Available now" announcements

**Best formats:** Reels (15s showcase), Carousel (3-5 detail shots), Stories (swipe-up/link)

**CTA:** DM to claim, link in bio, or direct checkout

---

### Pillar 2: Process (35%)
**Goal:** Highest reach. Process content gets shared and saved.

- Throwing on the wheel
- Trimming at leather-hard
- Glazing application
- Kiln loading / unloading
- "From lump to vase" transformations

**Best formats:** Reels (time-lapse + real-time mix), Carousels (step-by-step)

**CTA:** "Save this if you want to try this technique" / "What part of the process do you want to see more?"

---

### Pillar 3: Computation Experiments (15%)
**Goal:** Differentiate Cerafica from every other pottery account.

- Generative glaze patterns
- Code-driven form exploration
- Data viz + clay crossovers
- Parametric pottery concepts

**Best formats:** Reels (visual payoff first), Carousel (concept -> code -> clay)

**CTA:** "Send this to a coder who needs a hobby" / "Should I make a series of these?"

---

### Pillar 4: Cross-Domain Life (10%)
+*Goal:** Humanize the brand. Show there's a real person behind the work.

- Music practice / studio sessions
- Movement / exercise routine
- Data work / agent experiments
- Reading / influences

**Best formats:** Stories (casual, daily), occasional Reels

**CTA:** Minimal. Let it breathe.

---

### Pillar 5: Behind the Brand (10%)
**Goal:** Build trust and community.

- Studio life at Clay on First
- Kiln disasters and wins
- Shipping day
- Customer photos / UGC
- Artist story, identity

**Best formats:** Stories, Carousels, occasional Reels

**CTA:** "Show me your piece in its new home"

##3 Instagram Stories

Post **1-3 Stories daily**, even if brief:

- Morning: Works in progress
- Midday: Studio glimpse or poll
- Evening: Wrap-up or UGC reshare

**Story formats that work:**
- Polls: "Matte or glossy?" / "Tall or wide?"
- Sliders: "Rate this glaze 1-10"
- Questions: "What should I make next?"
- Countdowns: New drop announcements

## 4. The Caption Formula

Every caption follows this structure:

```
[HOOK] — What it is + key technique/material

[CONTEXT] — Process detail or story. What makes this piece special.

[SURPRISE] — Additional context, struggle, or unexpected result.

[CTA] — Question, save prompt, or send prompt.

[HASHTAGS] — 3-5 targeted tags
```

### Example

> Carbon trap Shino on a wheel-thrown bud vase
>
> This glaze is unpredictable — the black speckles come from carbon trapping in the kiln during reduction. I never know exactly how it'll turn out until the door opens.
>
> This one came out heavier on the speckles than I expected. I think it's my favorite from the firing.
>
> Save this if you love kiln surprises. What's your favorite — heavy speckle or light?
>
> #carbontrapshino #wheelthrown #longbeachartist #clayonfirst #ceramics

## 5. Hashtag Strategy

### Rule: 3-5 hashtags per post.

Instagram penalizes hashtag stuffing in 2026. Quality > quantity.

#### Tier 1: Local (always use 1-2)
```
#longbeachartist #clayonfirst #lbartist #socalartist
```

#### Tier 2: Technique (use 1-2)
```
#wheelthrown #handbuilt #carbontrapshino #cone10 #reductionfiring
```

#### Tier 3: Form/Style (use 1)
```
#ceramicvase #budvase #stoneware #modernceramics
```

### Banned Hashtags
These consistently underperform or attract bots:
- `#storysale` (historically low engagement for this account)
- `#handmade` (too broad, bot-heavy)
- `#art` (meaningless noise)
- Any hashtag with >10M posts unless it's a branded campaign

## 6. Content Formats by Platform

### Instagram Reels
- **Length:** 15-30 seconds
- **Hook:** First 2 seconds show the payoff
- **Audio:** Trending audio or original sound
- **Text overlay:** Minimal, monochrome, JetBrains Mono
- **Caption:** 300-500 characters

### Instagram Carousels
- **Slides:** 3-5 for process, 2-3 for finished pieces
- **First slide:** strongest image
- **Last slide:** CTA or details
- **Caption:** Educational, save-worthy

### Instagram Stories
- **Daily cadence:** 1-3 minimum
- **Best content:** polls, process clips, UGC, countdowns
- +*Links:** Use link stickers to shop or journal

##3 Journal (Blog)
- **Cadence:** 1 post every 1-2 weeks
- **Topics:** Kiln openings, glaze experiments, process deep-dives, artist statement
- **Length:** 400-800 words
- **Tone:** Educational, personal, scientific
- **CTA:** "See available work" / "Join the waitlist"

### Email
- **Cadence:** Bi-weekly for list, immediate for post-purchase
- **Topics:** New drops, behind-the-scenes, exclusive early access
- **Length:** 150-300 words
- **Tone:** Direct, warm, terminal-aesthetic

## 7. Voice Checklist (Every Post)

Before publishing, verify:

- [ ] No banned words (delve, tapestry, realm, embrace, elevate, etc.)
- [ ] Maximum 1 em dash
- [ ] Sentence lengths vary (short + long mixed)
- [ ] Includes at least one specific detail
- [ ] Includes a question or save/send CTA
- [ ] Sounds like something said out loud
- [ ] Would a potter roll their eyes at this?
