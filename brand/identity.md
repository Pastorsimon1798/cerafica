## Brand Name Origin

**CERAFICA = Cerámica + Gráfica + Facere**

| Hidden in CERAFICA | Root | Meaning | Practice |
|--------------------|------|---------|----------|
| **CER** | ceramica | clay | Ceramics |
| **GRA** | graphica | drawing, plotting | Painting, form, math, data viz |
| **FIC** | facere | to make, to do | Code, computation, generative systems |
| **CA** | calculare | to calculate | Data analysis, research agents |

## Brand Pillars

1. **Cross-domain** — Work spans ceramics, code, music, visual arts. Each discipline informs the others.
2. **Process-exposed** — The computation is part of the story, not hidden
3. **Identity-integrated** — Every perspective I bring shapes every piece. The work is personal.
4. **Designed objects** — Every piece is a true art object — beautiful, unique, one-of-a-kind. But it's also functional. Art you can live with.
5. **Anti-precious** — Made to be used, imperfection is feature
