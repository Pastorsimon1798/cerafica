# DM Sales Playbook for Cerafica

> The shift in 2026: DM conversations are where sales happen, not the feed.
> Last updated: 2026-03-12

## The DM Opportunity

| Fact | Impact |
|------|--------|
| DM shares = #1 algorithm signal | Accounts with active DMs get more reach |
| Instagram prioritizes conversations | Your content reaches more people if you're DMing |
| "Link in bio" friction kills sales | 80% drop-off when people have to leave the app |
| DM buyers convert 3-5x higher | They've already expressed intent |

## Your Current DM Process

Based on your bio: "All pieces for sale just ask!" and Etsy link

**Problem:** You're asking people to:
1. See the post
2. Go to your bio
3. Click the link
4. Find the piece on Etsy
5. Purchase

**Better:** Comment on post → DM automation → Close in conversation

## Comment-to-DM Triggers (Set Up Automation)

When someone comments these words, auto-DM them:

| Comment Trigger | Auto-DM Response |
|-----------------|------------------|
| "price" / "PRICE" / "$" | Send pricing + dimensions + shipping |
| "available" / "sold?" | Send availability + link to purchase |
| "ship" / "shipping" | Send shipping rates + packaging info |
| "pickup" / "local" | Send local pickup info (Long Beach / Clay on First) |
| "commission" / "custom" | Send commission process + pricing |
| "size" / "dimensions" | Send measurements + scale photos |

## Manual DM Response Templates

### Template 1: Price Inquiry (Most Common)

**Respond within 4 hours:**

> Hi! Thanks for your interest in the [piece name]!
>
> **Details:**
> - [dimensions] tall x [dimensions] wide
> - [glaze name/type]
> - [technique: wheel-thrown / hand-built]
>
> **Price:** $[XX]
> **Shipping:** $[X] (US) / Free pickup in Long Beach
>
> I accept Venmo, PayPal, or can list on Etsy if you prefer. Would you like me to hold it for you?
>
> Out of curiosity - what drew you to this piece? I love hearing what connects people to the work.

### Template 2: Multiple Pieces Interest

> Great question! Here's what I have available right now:
>
> [Piece 1] - $[X] - [1-2 word description]
> [Piece 2] - $[X] - [1-2 word description]
> [Piece 3] - $[X] - [1-2 word description]
>
> I can do a bundle price if you're interested in multiple pieces! Which ones are calling to you?

### Template 3: Shipping Question

> I ship from Long Beach, CA! Here's the breakdown:
>
> **US Shipping:**
> - Small pieces (mugs, small bowls): $8-12
> - Medium pieces (vases, planters): $12-18
> - Large pieces: $18-25
>
> **International:** Calculated at checkout on Etsy
>
> **Free local pickup:** Long Beach (can arrange at Clay on First studio)
>
> Everything is double-boxed with lots of padding. I've shipped 50+ pieces and never had a break! Would you like me to get you a shipping quote?

### Template 4: Commission Inquiry

> I love doing custom work! Here's how it typically works:
>
> **Timeline:** 4-8 weeks (drying, bisque, glaze, firing)
> **Pricing:** Starts at $[X] for [type], varies by size/complexity
> **Process:**
> 1. We discuss what you're envisioning
> 2. I send sketches / similar past work
> 3. 50% deposit to start
> 4. Photos before glazing for approval
> 5. Balance due when ready
>
> What did you have in mind? Happy to brainstorm together!

## Follow-Up Sequence (If No Response)

### Day 1: Initial response with details
(Use templates above)

### Day 3: Gentle nudge
> Hey! Just checking in - still interested in the [piece name]? I've had a couple people ask about it, so wanted to give you first dibs. No pressure either way!

### Day 7: Final check-in
> Hi! Wanted to follow up one more time on the [piece name]. If it's not the right fit, no worries at all - happy to let you know when I make similar pieces in the future. Let me know!

### Day 10+: Close the loop
Mark as resolved. Don't spam.

## DM Best Practices

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Respond within 4 hours | Let messages sit for days |
| Include price upfront | Make them ask twice |
| Ask a question to keep convo going | Send one-word answers |
| Offer multiple payment options | Only accept one method |
| Send additional photos if asked | Assume they saw everything |
| Mention local pickup option | Forget about local buyers |

## Payment Options

| Method | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| **Venmo** | Fast, most people have it | No buyer protection |
| **PayPal** | Buyer protection, professional | Fees (2.9% + $0.30) |
| **Etsy** | Trust, built-in shipping calc | 6.5% fee + listing fee |
| **Cash (local)** | No fees, no paper trail | Harder to track |

**Recommendation:** Offer Venmo for quick sales, Etsy for international or buyers who want protection.

## DMs = Algorithm Boost

When you have active DM conversations:

1. Instagram marks that person as "Close Connection"
2. Your future posts show higher in their feed
3. Your Stories get priority placement
4. You appear in their notifications more

**Strategy:** Start conversations, not just respond to them. Reply to Stories, comment on posts, then DM.

## Closing Checklist

Before closing any sale:

- [ ] Confirmed piece is still available
- [ ] Sent final photo/video of exact piece
- [ ] Confirmed shipping address or pickup method
- [ ] Sent payment request (Venmo/PayPal/Etsy link)
- [ ] Confirmed payment received
- [ ] Sent shipping notification with tracking
- [ ] Followed up after delivery ("How does it look in your space?")

## Post-Sale DM

**When piece arrives:**

> Hi! Just wanted to check in - did the [piece name] arrive safely? I'd love to see it in its new home if you're up for sharing a photo! No pressure though. Hope you're enjoying it!

**Why this matters:**
- Builds relationship for repeat buyers
- User-generated content for your Stories
- Catches any shipping issues early
