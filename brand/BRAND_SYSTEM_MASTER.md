## Brand Pillars

1. **Cross-domain** — Ceramics, code, music, and data inform each other.
2. **Process-exposed** — The computation and craft are part of the story.
3. **Identity-integrated** — Every piece is shaped by the artist's full perspective.
4. **Designed objects** — Beautiful, unique, one-of-a-kind art you can live with.
5. **Anti-precious** — Made to be used. Imperfection is a feature.
