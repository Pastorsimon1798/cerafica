## 🚨 ANTI-ROBOT RULES (Critical)

### Words NEVER to Use

These are instant AI tells that make captions feel fake:

**BANNED:**
- delve, delving, let's delve
- tapestry, rich tapestry
- realm, in the realm of
- landscape (as metaphor)
- embrace, embracing
- elevate, elevating
- navigate, navigating
- embark, embarked
- foster, fostering
- groundbreaking, invaluable, relentless
- furthermore, moreover, additionally

### Punctuation Rules

- **Maximum 1 em dash (—) per caption** - AI is addicted to these
- Prefer periods over dashes
- Use parentheses for asides (like this)

### Sentence Variety

AI writes uniform sentences. Humans vary wildly.

**Bad:** All sentences 6-8 words
**Good:** Mix of 3-word, 15-word, 8-word sentences

### Human Voice Markers

Include at least ONE of these per caption:
- Specific detail (dimension, time, attempt number)
- Process struggle ("finally got the foot right")
- Uncertainty ("I think this might be my favorite")
- Surprise ("didn't expect that color")
