THE PROCESS

Pipeline

STEP 01 / 04

THROWING

Recycled clay with custom inclusions. Centering on the wheel. The form emerges from rotation and pressure.

STEP 02 / 04

FORMING

No trimming. Every piece is hand-finished using intuition and experience. The shape emerges through feel.

STEP 03 / 04

GLAZING

Mixing and layering studio glazes into unexpected combinations. Every surface is an experiment.

STEP 04 / 04

FIRING

Bisque at ~1,800°F. Glaze fire to cone 10 (~2,350°F) in reduction. Iron goes warm, copper blushes red.
