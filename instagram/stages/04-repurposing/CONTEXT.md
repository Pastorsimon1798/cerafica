# Stage: Repurposing -- Stories & Reels

Extract more value from feed content.

## Inputs

| Source | What | Location |
|--------|------|----------|
| Captions | Source material | `../03-content/output/` |
| Weekly assets | Raw assets | `../01-input/output/` |
| Story templates | Story patterns | `references/story-templates.md` |
| Reel formulas | Video structures | `references/reel-formulas.md` |

## Process

1. Identify which feed posts can become stories
2. Generate story ideas for the week (post shares, studio moments, polls)
3. For video-capable assets, sketch reel concepts
4. Create repurposing brief

## Output

| File | Location |
|------|----------|
| Story ideas | `output/stories-[date].md` |
| Reel concepts | `output/reels-[date].md` (if applicable) |

**Story format**: Day-by-day table with story type, content, and notes.

**Reel format**: Per-reel breakdown with hook, body, CTA, and music suggestion.

## Checkpoint

Review story/reel plan:
- "Does this feel manageable for your time?"
- Adjust complexity based on capacity.

## Completion

This is the final stage. Your content pack is complete:
- Weekly assets documented
- Content calendar set
- Captions written
- Stories/reels planned

Ready to post throughout the week.
