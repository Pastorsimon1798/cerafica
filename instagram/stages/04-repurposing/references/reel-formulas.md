# Reel Formulas

Proven structures for ceramics reels.

## Formula 1: Start to Finish

**Length**: 15-30 seconds
**Best for**: Showing complete process

**Structure**:
- 0-3s: Hook -- clay hitting wheel or raw material
- 3-25s: Process montage -- throwing, trimming, glazing
- 25-30s: Reveal -- finished piece

**Music**: Trending audio or ASMR-style

---

## Formula 2: Before/After

**Length**: 10-15 seconds
**Best for**: Transformation reveal

**Structure**:
- 0-5s: Before -- unfired, raw, or work-in-progress
- 5-10s: Transition -- wipe or cut effect
- 10-15s: After -- finished piece, beautiful light

**Music**: Build-up then drop

---

## Formula 3: Close-Up Details

**Length**: 10-15 seconds
**Best for**: Texture, glaze, craftsmanship

**Structure**:
- Series of extreme close-ups
- Focus on texture, glaze variation, hand details
- No narrative, pure aesthetic

**Music**: Calm, atmospheric

---

## Formula 4: The Mistake

**Length**: 15-20 seconds
**Best for**: Authenticity, relatability

**Structure**:
- 0-5s: "What I planned" [ideal version]
- 5-15s: "What happened" [reality -- crack, wobble, fail]
- 15-20s: Resolution or humor

**Music**: Comedic or dramatic irony

---

## Formula 5: Timelapse

**Length**: 15-30 seconds
**Best for**: Showing time investment

**Structure**:
- Full process sped up 10-20x
- Text overlay with time ("4 hours in 20 seconds")
- Optional: final reveal at end

**Music**: Anything that fits mood

---

## Formula 6: Tutorial/Tip

**Length**: 30-60 seconds
**Best for**: Value-add, saves

**Structure**:
- 0-3s: Hook -- "Here's a trick for..."
- 3-45s: Step-by-step demonstration
- 45-60s: Result + CTA ("Save for later")

**Music**: Neutral background or voice-over

---

## Formula 7: Satisfying Loop

**Length**: 7-15 seconds
**Best for**: Meditative, high-share

**Structure**:
- Single satisfying action on loop
- Trimming, wire cutting, glaze dipping
- Seamless start/end for loop

**Music**: ASMR or trending audio

---

## Reel Planning Tips

| Tip | Why |
|-----|-----|
| Film vertically | Native format |
| Start with movement | Hook in first 2 seconds |
| Use text overlays | Many watch without sound |
| Follow trends carefully | Only if it fits your brand |
| Post 1-2x per week | Consistency over quantity |

## Reel vs. Story Decision

| Content | Best Format |
|---------|-------------|
| Complete process | Reel |
| Quick moment | Story |
| Tutorial | Reel |
| Poll/question | Story only |
| Transformation | Reel or Story sequence |
| Candid/random | Story |
