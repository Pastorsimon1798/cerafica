# Story Templates

Patterns for Instagram stories that complement your feed.

## Post Amplification

### New Post Share
- Share feed post to stories
- Add "New post" sticker or text
- Optional: add context or behind-the-scenes

### Post Teaser (Before Posting)
- Close-up detail of piece
- "Coming to feed" text
- Build anticipation

### Post Recap (End of Week)
- Collage or carousel of week's posts
- "This week in the studio"

---

## Engagement Stories

### Poll
```
[Image of two pieces/details]

"This one or that one?"
[Add poll sticker]
```

### Question Box
```
[Studio shot or work in progress]

"Ask me anything about [topic]"
[Add question sticker]
```

### Quiz
```
[Image]

"Guess how long this takes to make?"
[Add quiz sticker with options]
```

### Slider
```
[Image of satisfying moment or beautiful piece]

"How satisfying is this?"
[Add slider sticker]
```

---

## Process Stories

### Step-by-Step Sequence
1. Story 1: "Starting a new piece" [raw material]
2. Story 2: "Throwing" [action shot]
3. Story 3: "Details" [close-up]
4. Story 4: "Done for today" [result]

### Before/After
1. Story 1: "Before" [unfired/raw]
2. Story 2: "After" [finished]
3. Add swipe-up or link if applicable

### Time-Lapse Share
- Share sped-up process video
- Add text: "X hours in 15 seconds"

## Studio Life Stories

### Day-in-Studio
- Morning: coffee, studio setup
- Midday: work in progress
- Evening: result or mess

### Candid Moments
- Messy hands
- Kiln surprises
- Studio mascot/pet

### Personal Notes
- What you're listening to
- How you're feeling
- Gratitude moments

---

## Sales Stories

### Available Now
- Photo of available piece
- "Still available" text
- DM prompt

### Sold
- Photo of sold piece
- "Going to a new home"
- Thank you

### Collection Preview
- Multiple pieces in one story
- "Dropping [day]" or "DM for first dibs"

---

## Story Frequency Guide

| Story Type | Frequency |
|------------|-----------|
| Post share | Every post day |
| Engagement | 2-3x per week |
| Process | 1-2x per week |
| Studio life | As moments happen |
| Sales | When relevant |
