# Content Pillars

The four types of content that populate your Instagram.

## 1. Finished Work

**Purpose**: Sell available pieces

**When to use**:
- New work ready to sell
- Pieces that haven't sold yet (repost)
- Customer pickups (with permission)

**CTA**: Always include "DM to claim" or sales method

**Balance guideline**: 40-50% of feed posts

---

## 2. Process / Making

**Purpose**: Show craft, build appreciation, create connection

**When to use**:
- Throwing on the wheel
- Trimming, handles, details
- Glazing
- Kiln loading/unloading
- Before/after firing

**CTA**: Engagement-focused (comment, save, share)

**Balance guideline**: 25-35% of feed posts

---

## 3. Studio Life

**Purpose**: Humanize the maker, build relationship

**When to use**:
- Studio moments
- Tools and workspace
- Behind-the-scenes glimpses
- Day-in-the-life snippets

**CTA**: Conversational (questions, stories)

**Balance guideline**: 10-20% of feed posts

---

## 4. Atmospheric / Mood

**Purpose**: Aesthetic consistency, brand building

**When to use**:
- Detail shots
- Texture close-ups
- Light and shadow play
- Styled vignettes

**CTA**: Minimal or save-for-later

**Balance guideline**: 10-15% of feed posts

---

## Mixing Pillars

The best weeks combine multiple pillars. Examples:

**Sales-focused week**:
- 3 finished work posts
- 1 process post
- 1 studio life post

**Process-focused week**:
- 2 process posts
- 1 finished work post
- 1 atmospheric post

**Balanced week**:
- 2 finished work posts
- 2 process posts
- 1 studio/atmospheric post
