# Hook Patterns for Pottery Captions

> Research-based hooks adapted for ceramics/craft accounts.
> Updated: March 2026

---

## The 3-Second Rule

Your hook must land in the first 2-3 seconds (or first line).
- Algorithm rewards watch time
- Visual + text hooks work better than text alone
- Coordinate your words to what's shown

---

## Hook Categories

### 1. Curiosity Hooks

| Generic | Pottery Version |
|---------|-----------------|
| "Why is no one talking about this?" | "Why don't more potters use [glaze]?" |
| "This changed everything for me." | "Switching to [clay body] changed everything." |
| "I wish I knew this sooner." | "Wish I learned this trimming trick sooner." |
| "Don't post until you see this." | "Don't glaze another piece until you try this." |

**Pottery examples:**
- "The kiln gods were kind today."
- "This glaze combination shouldn't work. But it does."
- "Three months of work. One kiln opening."

### 2. Specific-Value Hooks

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| [X] + [specific detail] | "Tenmoku on B-Mix. The combo I didn't know I needed." |
| [Number] + [timeframe] | "47 seconds to trim this foot (actually 20 minutes)" |
| [Technique] + [result] | "Carbon trap Shino. The orange came from the reduction." |

**Pottery examples:**
- "Cone 10 reduction. 4 hours to throw. 30 seconds to ruin."
- "This bud vase? Attempt #4. The first 3 are in the shard pile."
- "Tenmoku + B-Mix clay = this combination I keep coming back to"

### 3. Problem-Solution Hooks

| Generic | Pottery Version |
|---------|-----------------|
| "Are you struggling with X?" | "Handles still wonky? Try this." |
| "Most people do this wrong." | "Most beginners trim too thin. Here's how I learned." |
| "Do this instead." | "Stop pulling handles dry. Do this instead." |

**Pottery examples:**
- "S-cracks ruining your pieces? Your compression is the problem."
- "Glaze running onto shelves? Three words: slower dip speed."
- "Lids not fitting after firing? Wax the gallery."

### 4. Vulnerability Hooks

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Failure admission | "This one was supposed to be a bowl." |
| Process struggle | "Attempt #7. Finally didn't wobble." |
| Surprise outcome | "Did not expect it to turn out this good." |

**Pottery examples:**
- "That kiln opening feeling when you're not sure what you'll get."
- "The crack happened during drying. I kept it anyway."
- "Not my best work but it's got personality."

### 5. Engagement Hooks

| Pattern | Pottery Example |
|---------|-----------------|
| A or B choice | "Matte or gloss on this one? Can't decide." |
| Rating request | "Rate this glaze combo 1-10, be honest." |
| Tag someone | "Tag someone who needs to see this glaze." |
| Double tap if | "Double tap if kiln openings give you anxiety." |

**Pottery examples:**
- "Which handle style? Traditional vs loop"
- "Drop a 🔥 if this glaze variation is your vibe"
- "Send to someone who loves a good Tenmoku"

### 6. Anti-Hooks (Minimalist)

Sometimes saying less works better.

**Pottery examples:**
- "Just a vase. A really good one though."
- "Tenmoku."
- "Made this today."

## Hook Templates for AI Generation

### Finished Piece Hooks
```
{glaze} on {clay}. {one specific observation}
{piece_type}. {technique}. {surprise detail}
{piece_type} with {glaze}. The {surface_quality} gets me every time.
```

### Process Hooks
```
From {start_state} to {end_state} in {real_time}
{technique} demo. {one tip worth knowing}
The {specific_part} is always the hardest part.
```

### Kiln/Firing Hooks
```
Kiln opening day. {emotion}
Came out of the kiln looking like {description}
{glaze} did not look like this going in.
```

### Sales Hooks
```
Looking for its forever home.
One of one. Won't make another exactly like this.
{piece_type} is available. DM to claim.
```

## Hooks to AVOID

### AI Generic Hooks
- "Let's dive into..." (banned word)
- "In the world of pottery..." (banned pattern)
- "Pottery is an art form that..." (too formal)
- "Discover the magic of..." (marketing speak)

### Overused Patterns
- "POV:" (overdone)
- "Tell me you're a potter without telling me..." (dead)
- "Nobody:" (old meme format)

## Implementation

When generating captions, select hook type based on content:

| Content Type | Best Hook Categories |
|--------------|---------------------|
| Finished piece | Specific-value, Vulnerability |
| Process video | Curiosity, Problem-solution |
| Kiln reveal | Vulnerability, Surprise |
| Sales post | Specific-value, Minimal |
| Carousel | Curiosity, Engagement |
