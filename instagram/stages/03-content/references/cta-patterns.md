# CTA Patterns

Call-to-action patterns for different post types.

## Sales CTAs

### Direct Purchase
- "DM to claim"
- "DM me to purchase"
- "Send a DM to bring this home"

### Claim System
- "Comment 'SOLD' to claim"
- "First to comment gets it"
- "Drop a [emoji] to claim"

### Multiple Pieces
- "Swipe through -- DM which one speaks to you"
- "Numbers 1-4 available. DM your pick."

### Soft Sell
- "Looking for its forever home. DM if interested."
- "Available in the shop. Link in bio."

---

## Engagement CTAs

### Questions
- "What's your favorite glaze color? Tell me below."
- "Morning studio or night owl -- which are you?"
- "What should I make next? Drop your ideas."

### Save/Share
- "Save this for your next studio session."
- "Send this to a ceramic friend."
- "Bookmark for later."

### Conversation
- "Tell me about your creative space."
- "What's on your studio playlist?"
- "Drop a [emoji] if you needed this today."

---

## Story CTAs

### Polls
- "This glaze or that?"
- "Keep it or sell it?"
- "Which handle style?"

### Engagement
- "Reply with your favorite piece from this week"
- "Questions about process? Ask away."

### DM Prompt
- "Want first dibs on new work? Turn on post notifications."
- "DM me 'POTTERY' to join the waitlist."

---

## CTA Selection Guide

| Post Type | CTA Style |
|-----------|-----------|
| Finished work (available) | Direct sales CTA |
| Finished work (sold) | Engagement or no CTA |
| Process | Engagement or save/share |
| Studio life | Conversational question |
| Atmospheric | Minimal or none |

## Frequency Guideline

- Not every post needs a CTA
- Sales posts: always include claim instructions
- Process posts: mix of CTA and CTA-free
- Atmospheric posts: often no CTA
