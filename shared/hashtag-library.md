## Strategy: 5-Slot Framework

Each of the 5 hashtag slots serves a **distinct purpose**. Never double up on purpose.

| Slot | Purpose | What It Does | Size Target |
|------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Niche** (what is this) | Puts you in a specific community | 50K-500K posts |
| 2 | **Technique** (how was it made) | Reaches people interested in process | 50K-200K posts |
| 3 | **Audience** (who is this for) | Connects with your target buyer/collector | 100K-500K posts |
| 4 | **Content type** (what format) | Helps algorithm categorize the format | 50K-200K posts |
| 5 | **Local** (where) | Reaches nearby community | 10K-50K posts |

### Why This Works

- Each tag describes a **different aspect** of the post (no overlap)
- Algorithm gets clear signals about content, audience, and format
- No wasted slots on redundant tags
- Local tag always included for IRL connections

## Rules of Thumb

1. **5 hashtags max** — Instagram enforces this
2. **Each slot = different purpose** — no overlap
3. **10K-500K post range** — sweet spot for nano accounts
4. **Rotate tags** — don't use the same 5 every post
5. **Hashtags in caption** (not comments) — 36% reach boost for accounts under 100K
6. **Match the post** — technique matches reality, format matches content type
7. **Local tag always** — #longbeachartist unless there's a specific reason not to
